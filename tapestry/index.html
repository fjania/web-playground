The day
I Knew
I was
an Adult
This is my
grandfather
The grandkids all call him
"Poppy"
He was, once,
a young man
too.
(but I'd never really considered that.)
For most of my life
(until I
was about 30)
I got the sense that
Poppy saw me...
as a
little
kid
Until one night
when my uncle Larry had me
and the grandparents
over for dinner.
I’d been vegan
for about 5 years at that point.
Uncle Larry surprised me
that night and made
the whole meal vegan.
Tofu omelettes
Red lentil salad
Brown rice pizza
It was a feast.
(For me)
Poppy was not as pleased.
I suggested he try
the tofu omelette
“they’re good” I said.
No, no.
He replied quickly.
I don’t think I’ll like those.
Poppy will eat
just about anything
and always wanted
me to try new foods growing up.
So I couldn’t possibly pass up
what would likely be
the only opportunity
I'd ever have to reuse
one of his famous lines on him...
But how do know you won’t like it
if you don’t try it?
This is where I
<smirk>
for being so clever.
He smiled
and chuckled just a bit...
then did that little wave that says
“Come over here,
I have a secret for you...”
I leaned in
and through a little bit of laughter
he whispered into my ear...
"Go fuck yourself."
This is where he
<smirks>
for being so wise.
That was the day
I knew
he saw me as an adult.
