Example Expected Steps
1. Load this page and set your zoom to normal, 100%
2. Place your cursor in front of the 't' in two
3. Press backspace to move the word 'two' up a line
4. The words 'one' and 'two' are next to each other


Steps to Reproduce
1. Load this page and set your zoom to something other than 100%, e.g. 90%
2. Place your cursor in front of the 't' in two
3. Press backspace to move the word 'two' up a line
4. The words 'one' and 'two' are next to each other, but in the
failing case, the word 'two' has been wrapped in a span tag which
appears to try and preserve the font size
